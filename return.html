Exchanges and Returns

Welcome to our Returns and Exchanges center. Here you will find all the information needed to return or exchange your purchase. Please follow the guidelines to ensure a smooth process.

Steps to Initiate a Return:
  1. Create your exchange or return here. If you are a registered user, log in to your NET‑A‑PORTER account under 'My Orders' and select the order number containing the item(s) you would like to return.
  2. Click on the "CREATE EXCHANGE/RETURN" button and choose the reason for return. If you’d like an exchange, select your preferred size.
  3. Check the box to agree with our Return Policy. If you have misplaced your return label, you can request a replacement in the 'My Orders' section in your NET‑A‑PORTER account. You will then receive an email with a label attached within the next 48 hours to the email address used to place your order.
  4. Sign the returns proforma invoice included in the box with your order. If you no longer have this, you can find a replacement PDF copy attached to your Dispatch Email. Seal your box before handing it to the courier.
  5. Return your purchase and drop off your return at a UPS Access Point of your choice.

Note: Returns are free if you drop off your purchase at a UPS Access Point using the pre-printed return label enclosed in the original order within 14 days of the date you received the order. Otherwise, if the items are sent back to us after 14 days, a fee of $9.95 + tax will be deducted from your refund. Such fee will not apply if you return the item because it is faulty.

If you choose not to use the pre-printed return label and return your purchase with a different courier, no return fee will be deducted from your refund. You will need to pay the return shipping costs agreed with your chosen shipping provider.

No return fee applies to exchanges.

Return Address: NET‑A‑PORTER DC2, 725 Darlington Avenue, Mahwah, NJ 07430, USA
